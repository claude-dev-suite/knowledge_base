## What this covers

Ark protocol: VTXOs (Virtual Transaction Outputs) batched off-chain with periodic on-chain settlement. ARKADE (Ark Labs) is the first mainnet implementation. ASP (Ark Service Provider) coordinates rounds; users keep unilateral exit.
