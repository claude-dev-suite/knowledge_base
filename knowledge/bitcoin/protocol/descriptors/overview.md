## What this covers

Output descriptors: BIP380-385 family. Descriptor language for declaring scriptPubKey templates with key origin info, ranges, checksums. Functions: pkh, wpkh, sh, wsh, tr, multi, sortedmulti, multi_a, addr, raw, combo, rawtr.
