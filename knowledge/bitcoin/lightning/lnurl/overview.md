## What this covers

LNURL: bech32-encoded HTTP URL fetched by wallet to enable payment flows. LUDs (LNURL specs): LUD-01 (encoding), LUD-04 (auth), LUD-06 (pay), LUD-09 (success action), LUD-12 (comment), LUD-16 (lightning address), LUD-21 (verify).
