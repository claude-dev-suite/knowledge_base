## What this covers

Painting 2D art for dynamic lighting: workflow for Unity 2D Lights / Godot CanvasLight, normal map authoring (Sprite Lamp / hand-painted), separation in layers (diffuse / normal / emissive / mask), self- shadowing in pixel art, day/night palette swaps vs realtime light mixing, glow / emissive layers.
