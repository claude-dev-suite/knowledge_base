## What this covers

Color palettes for 2D games: color theory practical (warm/cool, complementary, analogous), restricted palettes ready-made (PICO-8, GameBoy DMG, DB16, DB32, AAP-64, NES, C64), hue shifting (warm-cool ramps), palette swaps for character variants and time-of-day, indexed mode workflow, .gpl/.pal/.ase/.json export.

dev-suite `SKILL.md` linked above. Topic-specific Phase B articles
