# Code of Conduct

<!-- SPDX-License-Identifier: MIT -->

## Standards

We expect all contributors to behave professionally and respectfully. This means:

- Keeping discussions focused on the technical merit of contributions
- Accepting constructive feedback gracefully
- Being direct and honest without being unnecessarily hostile

## Enforcement

Violations may be reported by opening a GitHub issue or contacting the maintainers directly. Reports will be reviewed and acted upon at the maintainers' discretion.
